USACO.Guide Informatics Tournament

A competitive programming contest for high school students hosted by the Comeptitive Programming Initiative

Contest Information

  • Everyone can participate however only high schoolers are eligible for prizes (high school graduation month >= december 2024).
  • Contestants can register for one of two divisions (beginner or advanced) but not both.
  • Contestants that are either gold and above in USACO or 1700+ on Codeforces are required to compete in the advanced division.
  • In each division, contestants will compete individually to solve 7-8 problems in 3 hours.
  • The contest will be held on March 8th from 10:30 AM to 1:30 AM PDT.
